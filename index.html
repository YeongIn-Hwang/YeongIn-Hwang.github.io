Introduce Myself

I'm glad to see you! Welcome to my Homepage!

I'm YeongIn Hwang. Young, 23 years old, I completed my military service and am currently in my second year at Sejong University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence Data Science.

I have chosen a career path in game development utilizing artificial intelligence technology. Modern games typically offer predetermined responses, but I aim to advance this by creating games where players can engage in dialogue with various NPCs and experience true adventures. Bringing joy to many people is my dream and goal.

Of course, the journey ahead will be challenging. Developing AI technology, as well as acquiring in-depth expertise in various fields, will be essential. However, as the quote from "The Old Man and the Sea" suggests, humans are not born to give up. We will always strive towards our goals without surrendering.
